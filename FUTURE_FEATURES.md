## üì± Core Features (High Priority)

### 1. Trip History & Analytics
- **Trip Logs**: Har trip ka complete record (date, time, distance, duration, route)
- **Statistics Dashboard**: 
  - Total distance traveled
  - Average speed
  - Risk zones encountered
  - Safety score (based on driving behavior)
- **Trip Replay**: Past trips ko map pe replay karo
- **Export Reports**: PDF/CSV format mein trip reports

### 2. Real-time Traffic Integration
- **Live Traffic Data**: Google Maps/TomTom API se traffic info
- **Congestion Alerts**: Traffic jam ki warning
- **Alternative Routes**: Faster route suggestions
- **ETA Calculation**: Accurate arrival time prediction
- **Road Closures**: Construction/accident se band roads ki info

### 3. Advanced SOS Features
- **Emergency Contacts**: 5 contacts add karo jo emergency mein auto-notify honge
- **One-tap SOS**: Big red button for instant help
- **Live Location Sharing**: Emergency contacts ko real-time location share
- **Voice-activated SOS**: "Hey SmartSafe, emergency!" bolne pe activate
- **Medical Info**: Blood group, allergies, medical conditions store karo
- **Nearby Hospitals**: Closest hospital with directions

### 4. Community Features
- **User Reports**: Users accident zones, potholes, speed breakers report kar sakte hain
- **Crowdsourced Data**: Community se real-time hazard updates
- **Verification System**: Reports ko verify karne ka system
- **Reputation Score**: Active contributors ko badges/points
- **Social Sharing**: Friends ke saath trips share karo

### 5. Smart Notifications
- **Push Notifications**: Mobile app notifications
- **Voice Alerts**: Text-to-speech warnings (already implemented)
- **Vibration Alerts**: Phone vibrate for critical warnings
- **Smart Timing**: Alerts ko context-aware banao (speed, location, time)
- **Alert History**: Past alerts ka log
